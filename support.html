Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. If you subscribe to BudgetDaily Pro and enable iCloud Sync, your data is also stored in your private iCloud account. We never have access to your data.

How do I restore my data on a new device?

If you have iCloud Sync enabled, simply sign in with the same Apple Account on your new device and your data will sync automatically. You can also restore from a cloud backup in Settings → iCloud → Manage Backups.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your device to manage or cancel your BudgetDaily Pro subscription.

Can I share a budget with someone?

Yes. BudgetDaily supports budget sharing via iCloud. You can invite others to view your shared budgets directly from the app.
