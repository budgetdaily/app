How BudgetDaily works

A trusted ledger for
daily spending decisions.

BudgetDaily is built for people who want deliberate, private control. Keep your real accounts mirrored in the app, log transactions as they happen, and let budgets, recurring commitments, analytics, widgets, and backups work from that source of truth.

1

Mirror your accounts

Add the accounts, loans, and investments you want to track. The app becomes a private ledger that reflects your real-world money.

2

Log spending quickly

Record expenses, income, transfers, receipts, and split payments as they happen so your balances stay trusted.

3

Let budgets update

Categorized transactions automatically update your weekly, fortnightly, or monthly budgets. No separate budget bookkeeping.

4

Know today's limit

Daily Balance shows what you can safely spend now after rollover, overspending, and committed recurring expenses are accounted for.

The Daily Balance in plain English

Set a $70 weekly grocery budget and BudgetDaily starts you at $10 per day. If it is Wednesday and you have spent $18, you are $12 ahead of pace. If you have spent $42, you are $12 behind and the app shows what it takes to recover.
