Track Everything

Accounts, loans, investments, and every transaction in between.

Multiple Account Types Standard, loan, and investment accounts with today and projected balances.
40 Currencies Cross-currency transfers, adjustable exchange rates, and converted balances.
Recurring Transactions Subscription tracker with countdown badges, pause and resume, and confirmations.
Receipt Capture Snap and store receipts with any expense.
Split Expenses Equal or custom splits with repayment tracking.

Flexible Budgets Weekly, fortnightly, monthly, or custom periods with calendar alignment.

Daily Rate Pacing See exactly how much you can spend each day to stay within budget.
Budget Groups Organise budgets into groups for a cleaner overview.
Shared Budgets Share budgets with family or housemates via iCloud. Pro

Export & Backup CSV export & import. Auto cloud backup with iCloud. Pro
Home Screen Widgets Budgets, balances, and future transactions at a glance. Pro
